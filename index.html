Android
Foodinator
(March 2017)
Languages used: Android, Java, PHP, SQL
Foodinator is an android application which allows users (donors) to donate food and consumers (food banks and underprivileged people) to collect and distribute food with the help of volunteers. This application simplifies the process by allowing donors to make a request for food pickup at their convenient location, the app then identifies nearby volunteers and redirects them to the donors for food pickup which is then transported to the nearby food banks and later to the underprivileged people. This app serves as a bridge to solve the food crisis in cities where there is wastage of food and no means of transporting the food within food lifespan and avoid any food wastage by transporting from one location to another in quick and efficient way. It also utilizes the powerful machine learning techniques to analyze and classify the food to be picked up by the volunteer and distribute it efficiently to various food banks based on the needs and capacity of the food banks.

C++
Mirage
(April 2017)
Languages and Technologies used: C++, OpenCV, Microsoft Cognitive Services APIs
Mirage is a full Cognitive Photo application which detect your emotions and accordingly talks to you to proceed to the steps of taking the perfect picture and the generates auto captions for the photo. Firstly the application will detect you emotion and appropriately synthesis a speech based on your current emotion. Then come the photo cleaning part where a video (ie frames of pictures) of about 5-6 sec is recorded and fed to the system. The system builds a great noise free picture. This noise free picture is then fed to the server which uses 3 cognitive models to find the following: The person in the picture, his/her current emotion and the place where he/she is at that moment. Uses the output of all these we extrapolate the entire caption and tag it to the photo. The final photo is noise free and auto captioned.
